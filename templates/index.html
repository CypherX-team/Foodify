There is no doubt that Indian weddings are a huge event! Tons of food is prepared for the grand wedding celebrations, but does all that food get eaten? Ans is BIG NO! In fact, all that food is thrown away and wasted. The Foodify is a website created in order to lessen the wastage of food. Countless children sleep at night empty stomach. Our aim is to give the leftover food to NGO/ individual so that they could distribute to the people in need!

What is Foodify?

Foodify is the website created in order to lessen the food wastage. This will help to connect the manager/owner of banquet to the NGOs who further can distribute this food to the people in need.

Why Foodify?

There have been a lot of surveys conducted which proved after an event, there's a lot of food left which ultimately gets dumped.Our aim is to connect the owner/manager of banquet halls where such food is left to the NGOs who tend to distribute this food to the needy people.
